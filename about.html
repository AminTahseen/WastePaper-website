About & Our History!

Pioneers, Importer & Exporter of Wastepaper & Stocklot Trade.

We Specialize as an Importer and Exporter of many types of Wastepaper, Paper, Pulp, Prime Stocklot and associated Products. Since 1960, our family has been in the Paper business.

AR INTERNATIONAL is a trading house specialized in the trade of Wastepaper, Papers & Prime Paper & Board for packaging. Our business strategy is to build long-term relationships with customers and suppliers all around the world. The only and the most Valuable Assets we have are the people who make this happen. Thanks to our successful suppliers and our customers, we have been able to build business relationships that make us one of the most successful companies in our industry.
